Mentors' Labs


The “Mentors' Lab” was developed by Tell NP Advisers, an innovative outplacement company based in Geneva, Switerland. Our goal is to allow start-ups to showcase their businesses and for mentors to propose their help to these start-ups! Mentor a start-up! Your start-up can be based anywhere in the world, our mentors can also be located everywhere in the world! Mentors agrees to give time “pro-bono” (for free) to start-ups in need for help in specific areas: Marketing, Communication, Sales, Finance, Admin, Supply Chain, Purchasing, Fundraising, Digital, Strategy and other fiels for short terms periods (from 2 to 6 hours a week, as long as they want)... For a mutual benefit, our mentors can showcase these new experiences on their professional networks and CVs. Invite new startups and mentors to join this amazing journey!
